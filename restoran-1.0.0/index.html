OUR STORY

It is Kadalas that transformed the place into a space where everyone enjoys their own space. Kadalas, one of the popular sea-viewing cafes in Kerala situated at South Beach, has introduced a distinctive space to the cultural sphere of Calicut. The space is designed to become a subject that could interact with its guests. Its audience s privileged to opt for spaces in accordance with their mood. Over the course of time, it has been identified as the most popular gathering location in Calicut.


OUR TEAM

Strategic and critical thinking of the Managing Director, Ahammed Shihab and the solemn support of three partners, namely Abdul Haseeb, Suhas Ahamed and Muhammed Shahin, smoothened the trajectory of Kadalas. His entrepreneurial skills are sharpened by his experience handling multiple restaurant franchises from around the globe for a Qatar-based MNC.
