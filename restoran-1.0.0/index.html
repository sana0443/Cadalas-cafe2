About Us

Welcome to Kadalas Tasty Tales

Nestled by the serene waves and golden sands, Kadalas Cafe is your ultimate retreat, blending breathtaking ocean views with unforgettable culinary experiences. Whether you seek a tranquil seaside escape or a vibrant urban setting, we bring you the best of both worlds with our Sea View Cafe at Calicut Beach and Urban Cafe in Trivandrum.

At Cadalas Sea View Cafe, the rhythmic melody of the waves sets the perfect backdrop for a leisurely morning coffee, a sumptuous seafood feast, or a peaceful sunset moment. With fresh, locally sourced ingredients and a menu inspired by coastal flavors, every bite is a celebration of the sea.

For those who thrive in the heart of the city, Kadalas Urban Cafe in Trivandrum offers a modern, cozy ambiance where great food meets lively conversations. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick coffee break, a business lunch, or an evening unwind, our urban space brings the same warmth and excellence that define Cadalas..

From the tranquil shores of Kadalas Sea view cafe in Calicut Beach , Kadalas Cafe welcomes you to experience the perfect harmony of relaxation, indulgence, and culinary artistry.
