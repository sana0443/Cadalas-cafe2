STORY

At Kadalas Café, we believe dining is more than just food—it’s an experience.

Born by the sea in Calicut in 2018, Kadalas sea view cafe carries the rhythm of the waves in its soul - a space where flavours dance with the ocean breeze, where laughter lingers like salt in the air, and where every meal feels like a story waiting to be told.

In 2024, Kadalas Urban Café opened its doors in the heart of Trivandrum, marking an evolution of our passion for food and hospitality. As the city’s biggest café, it brings a fusion of global flavors, timeless classics, and bold innovations served in an inviting and elegant setting.

But beyond the food, Kadalas is about moments—the quiet ones, the celebratory ones, the ones shared between old friends and new. It’s about genuine connections, warm hospitality, and the simple joy of a meal made with care. Here, time slows down, conversations flow, and flavours linger a little longer.

Welcome to Kadalas Café—an experience woven with passion

TEAM

Kadalas’ journey has been shaped by the strategic vision of Ahammed Shihab, supported by Abdul Haseeb, Suhas Ahamed, and Muhammed Shahin. With extensive experience in working with global restaurant franchises, Shihab brings deep expertise in F&B operations, strategic expansion, and brand positioning. His insights enable Kadalas to evolve with market trends while preserving its unique identity. Complementing this vision, the team’s strong background in the FMCG sector provides valuable expertise in market dynamics, consumer behavior, and operational efficiency.

At its core, Kadalas thrives on the creativity of expert chefs who craft authentic, innovative flavors and a seasoned managerial team that ensures seamless operations and exceptional guest experiences. This synergy of leadership, culinary artistry, and operational excellence defines Kadalas as a brand known for quality, creativity, and hospitality.
